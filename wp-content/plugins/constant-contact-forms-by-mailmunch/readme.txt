== Description ==

> Constant Contact Forms by MailMunch allows you to painlessly add Constant Contact sign up forms to your WordPress site. The form captures the lead and sends it to specific Constant Contact lists automatically. You can set it to no op-in, opt-in, or double opt-in. Simply sign up and connect your Constant Contact account into the plugin admin settings and it will pull in all your Constant Contact lists. From there you can choose the lists you want to make forms for.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get started using it.

= Version 2.8+ =
1. Unzip our archive and upload the entire `constantcontact-mailmunch` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Look for "Constant Contact" in the WordPress admin menu and click it
4. Sign up or sign in to MailMunch account
5. Create your first Constant Contact optin form
6. Choose the Constant Contact form type: Popover, Embedded or Top Bar
7. Connect Constant Contact and choose a list
8. Start collecting user emails and growing your subscriber list!

= Advanced =
If you are using Wordpress v3.0 or higher, you can use the short-code given during the optin form creation. It will look something like this:
` [mailmunch-XYZ] `

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What in the world is Constant Contact? =
[Constant Contact](http://www.constantcontact.com/email-marketing2/overview) is full of useful, powerful email marketing features that are easy to use whether you're an email marketing expert, or a small business just getting started.

To learn more, just check out: [Constant Contact](http://www.constantcontact.com/email-marketing2/overview)

= Do I need to have a Constant Contact Account? =
Yes, you can register for one for free at [Constant Contact](http://www.constantcontact.com/ "Constant Contact Signup").

= Do I need a MailMunch account? =
You will need a MailMunch account if you want to use any of our premium features. It's 100% free to sign up and you can have unlimited Constant Contact forms for free.

= Do I need to have lists already set up in Constant Contact? =
Yes, you have to have at least 1 list set up in Constant Contact. 

= What do I do first? =
The first step is to activate the plugin and sign up. Then we will guide you through the process of connecting your Constant Contact account and choosing the list. This is done on the WordPress admin "Constant Contact" menu page.

= Do I need Constant Contact's API Key? =
You don't have to worry about that. We will guide you through the process.

= How do I add my Constant Contact lists? =
After you connect your Constant Contact account, the plugin will fetch all of your Constant Contact list information and load it into the plugin admin. You can simply choose the lists you want to make forms for.

= How do I add the Constant Contact sign up forms to my site? =
We offer a variety of Constant Contact forms: Popups, Embedded, Top Bar, etc. You can choose which pages, posts or categories to show it on. You can also use a shortcode to add a Constant Contact form to a specific area in a page or post. Everything is done visually.

= Does this plugin slow down my site? =
Absolutely not. Constant Contact by MailMunch is designed with high performance and scalability in mind. It's completely asynchronous and hosted on Amazon's highly scalable infrastructure. Some of our customers have very high traffic websites.

= How many more emails will list builder help me get? =
Most people see a 100% to 500% growth in their Constant Contact subscribers.
